Exhaustive Diagnostics

Our Four-Stage Refurbish Standards

We don't just resell stock. Our engineers systematically strip, inspect, clean, and verify every single asset to ensure mechanical performance meets original factory specs.

01

Intake Diagnostic

Pallets are unboxed and registered by barcode. Every component is subjected to a primary load check: battery capacity levels, thermal heating coils, motherboard hash rates, and frequency response.

02

Medical-Grade Clean

Cosmetic shell housings are micro-polished to remove scuffs. Hygiene items (toothbrushes) are placed in state-of-the-art medical UV-C sanitizers; headsets receive fresh earcups; hairdryers get mesh filter cleanouts.

03

Component Swap

Any battery below 90% health is replaced with premium long-life cells. Failing ASIC hashing chips, faulty heater switches, and audio driver arrays are replaced with brand new OEM units.

04

Final Audit

A rigorous final quality control loop is performed. Toothbrushes are factory sealed; headsets are audio balanced; miners undergo a 72-hour continuous burn-in load; then, Grade-A packaging is finalized.

Common Diagnostics & Local FAQ

❓ Why do Bose QuietComfort headphones stop charging?

This is typically triggered by a locked battery management thermistor or micro-fuse burnout on the main charging board. Our Vaughan audio lab repairs these components under microscope-soldering, restoring full charging cycles without needing a whole motherboard replacement.

❓ What are common signs an ASIC miner hashboard is failing?

If your dashboard reports missing ASIC chips, high operating temperature alerts on specific chains, or a severe drop in hashing rate, your hashboard has burnt capacitors or failing hash chips. Our Ontario hardware lab performs exact heat-gun chip reflowing and capacitor replacements.

❓ Why does static occur in Skullcandy Crusher bass sliders?

Dust and moisture build up on the potentiometer track over time, causing crackling or bass loss. We perform a complete solvent ultrasonic cleaning of the slider assembly, or desolder and replace the slide potentiometer with a brand new OEM part.

❓ How do Vaughan drop-offs and Ontario shipping works?

You can schedule a physical drop-off at our Vaughan headquarters (GTA) or ship your hardware securely via Canada Post. We issue detailed diagnostic tracking codes, keeping you fully updated on repair status from intake audit to stress-test approval.
