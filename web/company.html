Exhaustive Diagnostics

Our Four-Stage Refurbish Standards

We don't just resell stock. Our engineers systematically strip, inspect, clean, and verify every single asset to ensure mechanical performance meets original factory specs.

01

Intake Diagnostic

Pallets are unboxed and registered by barcode. Every component is subjected to a primary load check: battery capacity levels, thermal heating coils, motherboard hash rates, and frequency response.

02

Medical-Grade Clean

Cosmetic shell housings are micro-polished to remove scuffs. Hygiene items (toothbrushes) are placed in state-of-the-art medical UV-C sanitizers; headsets receive fresh earcups; hairdryers get mesh filter cleanouts.

03

Component Swap

Any battery below 90% health is replaced with premium long-life cells. Failing ASIC hashing chips, faulty heater switches, and audio driver arrays are replaced with brand new OEM units.

04

Final Audit

A rigorous final quality control loop is performed. Toothbrushes are factory sealed; headsets are audio balanced; miners undergo a 72-hour continuous burn-in load; then, Grade-A packaging is finalized.
